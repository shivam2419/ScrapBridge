-About ScrapBridge

Making a revolution in Trash



Welcome to ScrapBridge, your dedicated partner in tackling the global e-waste crisis. Our mission is to promote sustainable waste management practices, raise awareness about the environmental and health impacts of e-waste, and provide practical solutions for responsible disposal and recycling of electronic devices.

Our Mission

At ScrapBridge, we are committed to: Educating the public about the dangers of improper waste disposal and the benefits of recycling. Facilitating the safe and responsible recycling of electronic devices through accessible and convenient recycling programs. Advocating for stronger regulations and policies to ensure sustainable e-waste management and reduce environmental impact...Read more
